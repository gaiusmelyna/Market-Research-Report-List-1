<p><strong>The Camshaft Lifters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet Camshaft</li><li>Roller Camshaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camshaft lifters market primarily consists of two types: flat tappet camshafts and roller camshafts. Flat tappet camshafts feature a flat surface that rides directly on the lifter, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making them popular in older engines. In contrast, roller camshafts utilize a cylindrical roller on the lifter, reducing friction and wear, enhancing performance and durability, making them more suitable for modern high-performance engines. Each type serves distinct applications based on engine requirements and performance goals.</p></p>
