<p><ul><li>Sensitive Isolator</li><li>Insensitive Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic isolators are devices that allow light to pass in one direction while preventing reverse transmission, enhancing system performance. The market is divided into sensitive and insensitive isolators. Sensitive isolators are designed to operate effectively at low power levels, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as telecommunications and sensing. In contrast, insensitive isolators handle higher power levels and are typically used in industrial or robust environments, managing increased risk of damage from back reflections.</p></p>
