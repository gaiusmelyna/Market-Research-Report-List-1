<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lanthanum target market is categorized into two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat surfaces where lanthanum is deposited for applications like thin film production in electronics and optics. They provide uniform thickness and are often used in processes requiring precise layer control. Rotating targets, on the other hand, offer continuous use by spinning during sputtering, promoting even wear and longer lifespan. This type is preferred in high-volume manufacturing for consistent deposition and efficiency in producing advanced materials.</p></p>
