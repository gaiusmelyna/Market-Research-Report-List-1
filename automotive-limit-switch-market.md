<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Limit Switch Market is categorized into two main types: Contact Type and Non-Contact Type. Contact Type limit switches operate using physical contact to indicate the position of a moving part, providing reliability in various automotive applications. Non-Contact Type limit switches use technologies like magnetic fields or infrared signals to detect position without physical touch, offering benefits such as reduced wear and enhanced longevity. Both types play crucial roles in safety and automation within modern vehicles.</p></p>
