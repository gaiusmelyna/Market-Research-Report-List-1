<p><strong>The Automotive Stampings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Stamping</li><li>Hot Stamping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive stampings market is primarily divided into two main types: cold stamping and hot stamping. Cold stamping involves shaping metal sheets at room temperature, making it suitable for high-volume production of precision parts with excellent surface finish. In contrast, hot stamping involves heating metal to high temperatures before forming, resulting in stronger, lightweight components ideal for safety-critical applications. Both processes are essential in the automotive industry, catering to different requirements for durability, weight, and design complexity.</p></p>
