<p><p>The enzyme preparation market is categorized into six main types based on enzymatic functions: </p><p>1. **Oxidoreductases** facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, crucial in metabolism. </p><p>2. **Transferases** transfer functional groups between molecules, aiding in biosynthesis. </p><p>3. **Hydrolases** catalyze hydrolysis reactions, breaking down substrates with water, essential in digestion. </p><p>4. **Isomerases** convert molecules into their isomers, supporting metabolic pathways. </p><p>5. **Lyases** break chemical bonds by means other than hydrolysis or oxidation, involved in various biosynthetic processes. </p><p>6. **Ligases** join two molecules with the consumption of ATP, playing a critical role in DNA replication and repair.</p></p>
