<p><p>The avalanche airbags market is primarily divided into two types: mono avalanche airbags and dual avalanche airbags. Mono avalanche airbags consist of a single inflatable bag designed to enhance visibility and flotation during an avalanche. In contrast, dual avalanche airbags feature two separate bags, providing increased stability and a higher chance of staying on the surface. Both types cater to backcountry adventurers, offering protection and safety, with the choice depending on individual preferences, conditions, and specific usage requirements.</p></p>
