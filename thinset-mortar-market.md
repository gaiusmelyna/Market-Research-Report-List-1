<p><strong>The Thinset Mortar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-set Mortar</li><li>Latex-portland Cement Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Thinset Mortar market comprises primarily two types: Dry-set Mortar and Latex-Portland Cement Mortar. Dry-set Mortar, a cement-based adhesive, is preferred for its versatility and ease of use in tile installations. It requires the addition of water for activation. In contrast, Latex-Portland Cement Mortar includes latex additives, enhancing adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance. This type is ideal for challenging environments, such as wet areas. Both types support various applications in flooring and wall tiling, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
