<p><p>Waterjet cutting machines are primarily categorized into two types: pressurized water and abrasive mixture waterjet cutting machines. Pressurized waterjet cutting machines utilize high-pressure water streams to cut through materials, making them suitable for softer materials like foam or rubber. In contrast, abrasive mixture waterjet cutting machines combine high-pressure water with abrasive particles, allowing for cutting through harder materials, such as metals and stone. Both types offer versatility, accuracy, and a clean cutting process across various industrial applications.</p></p>
