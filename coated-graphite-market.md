<p><p>The coated graphite market encompasses various types, including metal carbide-coated, silicon carbide (SiC) coated, pyrolytic coated, and others. Metal carbide-coated graphite provides enhanced hardness and wear resistance, suitable for high-temperature applications. SiC coated graphite offers excellent thermal and electrical conductivity, ideal for semiconductor and aerospace industries. Pyrolytic coated graphite features a uniform, high-performance layer that enhances corrosion resistance and durability. Other coatings may include polymer or oxide layers, catering to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
