<p><p>PTFE dispersed resin powder is categorized into three market types based on compression ratios: low, medium, and high. Low compression ratio powders are typically used for applications requiring flexibility and good flow, such as coatings and seals. Medium compression ratio powders balance performance and processability, suitable for various industrial applications. High compression ratio powders offer superior durability and wear resistance, making them ideal for demanding environments like automotive and aerospace components. Each type serves specific needs within the PTFE market.</p></p>
