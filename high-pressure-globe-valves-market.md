<p><p>The high pressure globe valves market comprises several types, each designed for specific applications. Z-type high pressure globe valves feature a straight flow path, minimizing turbulence and pressure drop. Y-type globe valves have an angled body that allows for smoother flow, reducing friction losses. Angle-type globe valves direct the flow at a 90-degree angle, offering compact design advantages. Other types may include specialized or custom valves tailored for unique industrial needs. Each type serves critical roles in fluid regulation under high pressure.</p></p>
