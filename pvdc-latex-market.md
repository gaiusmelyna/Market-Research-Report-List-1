<p><strong>The PVDC Latex Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion PVDC Latex</li><li>Solution PVDC Latex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDC latex market is primarily categorized into two types: emulsion PVDC latex and solution PVDC latex. Emulsion PVDC latex is a water-based product used for coatings and films, offering excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases, making it ideal for packaging applications. Solution PVDC latex, on the other hand, is a solvent-based version that provides enhanced adhesion and gloss, commonly used in industrial coatings and adhesives. Both types serve diverse applications across various industries, including food packaging and construction.</p></p>
