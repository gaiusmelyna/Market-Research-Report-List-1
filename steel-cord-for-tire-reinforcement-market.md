<p><p>The Steel Cord for Tire Reinforcement market includes three main types based on tensile strength: Normal Tensile (NT), High Tensile (HT), and Super Tensile (ST). NT steel cords provide standard strength for general tire applications. HT steel cords offer enhanced durability and performance, suitable for high-load and high-speed tires. ST steel cords deliver superior tensile strength, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications and off-road tires, ensuring better traction and longevity under extreme conditions.</p></p>
