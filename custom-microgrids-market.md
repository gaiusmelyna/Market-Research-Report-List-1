These microgrids enhance energy reliability, efficiency, and sustainability, while enabling greater control over energy generation and consumption. </p><p>The Custom Microgrids Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.9% during the forecast period. Additionally, there is a growing emphasis on smart technologies and digitalization, enabling real-time monitoring and management.
