<p><p>The body armor market is categorized into several types based on materials used. Steel armor is durable and offers high protection but is heavier. UHMWPE (Ultra-High-Molecular-Weight Polyethylene) is lightweight, flexible, and resistant to impact. Aramid fibers, like Kevlar, provide excellent protection with moderate weight, ideal for soft armor. Composite ceramics blend materials for enhanced protection and reduced weight, commonly used in hard armor plates. Each material has unique attributes suited for varying levels of ballistic protection and wearability.</p></p>
