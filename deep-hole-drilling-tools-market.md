<p><p>The deep hole drilling tools market encompasses various technologies tailored for efficient drilling of deep, precision holes. Gun drilling utilizes a simple, long, straight tool designed with a single cutting edge for deep holes. BTA (Boring Tungsten Alloy) and STS (Single Tube System) methods involve the use of a double-tube structure, enhancing chip removal and coolant delivery. Other methods may include techniques like trepanning and reaming, each serving specific industrial applications to meet diverse requirements in machining and manufacturing.</p></p>
