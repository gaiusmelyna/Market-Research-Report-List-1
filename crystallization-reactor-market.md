<p><p>Crystallization reactors are specialized equipment used in the chemical and pharmaceutical industries to produce crystalline materials. The horizontal type features a long, cylindrical design that facilitates uniform heat and mass transfer, ideal for bulk processes. Conversely, vertical reactors utilize a tower-like structure, promoting efficient solvent evaporation and crystal growth in a compact space. The choice between horizontal and vertical designs often depends on factors such as production volume, space constraints, and desired crystallization efficiency.</p></p>
