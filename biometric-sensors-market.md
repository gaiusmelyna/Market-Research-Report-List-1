<p><p>Biometric sensors market comprises various types, each utilizing different technologies for authentication. Capacitive sensors detect user touch through changes in electrical capacitance, ideal for fingerprint scanning. Optical sensors capture detailed images of fingerprints or irises, leveraging light for recognition. Thermal sensors identify unique patterns through heat emitted by the body. Ultrasound sensors use sound waves to create a 3D profile of a fingerprint or face. Lastly, electric field sensors measure variations in electric fields for the authentication of body characteristics.</p></p>
