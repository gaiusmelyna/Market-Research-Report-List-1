<p><p>Laser Galvo scanners are categorized based on their axes of movement. Single-axis scanners rotate along one axis, typically used for simple applications like laser marking or engraving. Two-axis scanners can pivot on both horizontal and vertical axes, providing greater flexibility and precision for more complex tasks, such as laser cutting and 3D engraving. Three-axis scanners offer the most versatility, enabling movement in three dimensions, ideal for high-precision applications in industries like manufacturing, medical, and aerospace where intricate detailing is required.</p></p>
