<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Electrostatic Chucks ESC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer electrostatic chuck (ESC) market comprises two primary types: Coulomb type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) type. The Coulomb type uses electrostatic forces generated by voltage to secure wafers during processing, offering simplicity and effectiveness. In contrast, the JR type employs a combination of dielectric materials and electrostatic forces that enhance clamping stability and provide better temperature uniformity. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing for optimizing wafer handling and processing precision, directly influencing productivity and yield.</p></p>
