<p><p>The steel wire rope sling market is categorized into ungulvanized and galvanized types. Ungalvanized wire ropes are made from raw steel and are prone to corrosion, making them suitable for indoor applications with minimal exposure to moisture. In contrast, galvanized wire ropes are coated with a layer of zinc, enhancing their resistance to corrosion and increasing durability. This makes galvanized slings more suitable for outdoor and harsh environments, where longevity and strength are critical for heavy lifting and rigging applications.</p></p>
