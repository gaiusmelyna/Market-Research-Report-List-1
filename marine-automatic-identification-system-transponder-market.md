<p><p>The Marine Automatic Identification System (AIS) transponder market comprises two primary types: Class A and Class B Marine AIS. Class A AIS is mandatory for large vessels, transmitting detailed navigational data like position and speed, and operates with greater power and range. Conversely, Class B AIS is designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, offering less comprehensive data and shorter transmission ranges. Both systems enhance maritime safety and situational awareness, facilitating better communication and monitoring at sea.</p></p>
