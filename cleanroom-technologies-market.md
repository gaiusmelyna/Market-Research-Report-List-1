<p><p>Cleanroom technologies encompass three primary market types: hard wall cleanrooms, standard cleanrooms, and soft wall cleanrooms. Hard wall cleanrooms feature rigid, permanent structures ensuring optimal contamination control and durability, making them ideal for pharmaceuticals and aerospace. Standard cleanrooms offer flexibility in design and modularity, suitable for various industries with medium cleanliness requirements. Soft wall cleanrooms use flexible plastic or fabric barriers, allowing easy setup and mobility, making them ideal for less critical applications like electronics and research laboratories.</p></p>
