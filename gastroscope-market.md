<p><p>The gastroscope market comprises three main types: flexible gastroscopes, transnasal gastroscopes, and rigid gastroscopes. Transnasal gastroscopes offer a less invasive alternative by entering through the nose, minimizing discomfort during procedures. Each type addresses distinct clinical needs, catering to various diagnostic and therapeutic procedures in gastroenterology.</p></p>
