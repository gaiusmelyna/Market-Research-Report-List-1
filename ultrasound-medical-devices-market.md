<p><p>The ultrasound medical devices market is primarily divided into two types: diagnostic and therapeutic ultrasound. Diagnostic ultrasound involves imaging techniques that use high-frequency sound waves to visualize internal body structures, aiding in the diagnosis of various conditions such as pregnancies and organ abnormalities. Therapeutic ultrasound utilizes sound waves for treatment purposes, including tissue destruction, pain relief, and physical therapy. Both types are essential in modern medicine, providing non-invasive solutions for patient care and improved clinical outcomes.</p></p>
