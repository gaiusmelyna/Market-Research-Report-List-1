<p><p>The breast silicone implants market comprises two main types: traditional silicone breast implants and cohesive silicone gel breast implants. Traditional silicone implants contain a silicone shell filled with a viscous silicone gel, providing a natural feel and appearance. Cohesive silicone gel implants, often referred to as "gummy bear" implants, feature a thicker gel that maintains its shape even if the shell is damaged. Both types cater to different patient preferences for aesthetics, safety, and feel, influencing choices in breast augmentation procedures.</p></p>
