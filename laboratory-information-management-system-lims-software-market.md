<p><p>The Laboratory Information Management System (LIMS) software market comprises two primary deployment types: on-premise and on-cloud. On-premise LIMS is installed directly on a laboratory's servers, offering enhanced control over data security and direct management but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Conversely, on-cloud LIMS is hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, easier access, and lower initial costs, as users pay subscription fees. Each type caters to different operational needs, budget constraints, and data management preferences of laboratories.</p></p>
